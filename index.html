The Grove

Your personal sleep sound sanctuary.

Immerse yourself in a carefully curated collection of nature sounds and ambient atmospheres. The Grove lets you create your perfect relaxation soundscape: rain, thunderstorms, ocean waves, crackling campfires and much more. Blend multiple tracks, set a sleep timer and even pair stunning visuals with audio through Immersion Mode.

🌧️
Nature sounds — rain, thunder, ocean waves, forest & more
🔥
Ambient atmospheres — campfire, indoor spaces, cosy environments
🎨
Custom mix builder — blend tracks into your personal soundscape
🎥
Immersion Mode — full video + audio relaxation experience
⏰
Sleep timer — automatically stops after you fall asleep
Free — No IAP
🛠 Flutter
🔒 No data collected
🍎 App Store ▶ Google Play
“

Most apps offering nature sounds lock the majority of their library behind steep paywalls, leaving users with only 3–4 free sounds and ads on top of that. We thought that was unfair. So we built The Grove: 100% free, zero ads, over 80 sounds and every feature unlocked from day one.

🍎 iOS Exclusive

Flora

A new flower blooms every day, right on your Home Screen.

Flora is a beautiful iOS application that celebrates the diversity of the plant kingdom. Each day a brand-new flower is revealed directly on your Home Screen through native iOS Widgets. Discover stunning botanical photography, learn about each species, and let your lock screen turn into a living garden that changes with the seasons.

🌸
Daily flower — a new bloom showcased every single day
🪟
Native iOS Widgets — Home Screen & Lock Screen support
📚
Botanical details — species info, origin & curiosities
✨
Minimal design — elegant UI built entirely in Swift & SwiftUI
Free — No IAP
🛠 Swift / SwiftUI
🍎 iOS only
🍎 App Store
“

Can you name more than 10 flowers? We couldn't either. That question sparked Flora: a tiny daily ritual that lets you discover a new bloom every morning right on your Lock Screen or Home Screen widget. Each flower comes with a short description, an interesting fact and tips on how difficult it is to grow.

Hair Health

AI-powered scalp analysis & therapy tracker.

Hair Health is your personal hair care companion. Track your scalp condition over time, log ongoing treatments such as Minoxidil and other therapies, and leverage an AI-powered scan that provides an approximate analysis of your scalp health. Not a medical device. Monitor your Norwood Scale progression and stay consistent with your routine.

🤖
AI Scalp Scan — camera-based analysis powered by machine learning
💊
Therapy tracker — log Minoxidil, supplements & other treatments
📈
Norwood Scale monitor — visualise your progression over time
📅
Daily streaks — stay consistent with personalised reminders
🏆
Rewards system — earn points by maintaining healthy habits
Free — No IAP
📢 AdMob Ads
🛠 Flutter

ℹ️ Ads are shown to cover AI API costs — no purchases required.

🍎 App Store
“

Every competing hair-health app hides AI scans behind a paywall. We followed the same philosophy as The Grove: no feature should cost money. To cover the AI API costs we show short, non-intrusive ads through AdMob, so you always get the full experience for free.
